Atom Project

This was the first time I self-initiated a project out of a desire to educate and teach others. My main goal was to build a solid and accurate foundation of what exactly the atomic model is and how it works. I also tried to address certain misconceptions many people believe, such as an updated and accurate representation of the electron field and the smallest level of particles known as quarks.

Nuclear Energy

This project marked a crucial turning point since it was the first where I had to do significant research and thought even before starting to plan the layout. I am extremely proud of the finished product as it accurately and simply explains very complicated topics. I saw a huge shift in layout design as further experience helped my placement and design choices.
